75th Republic Day

India will be celebrating its 75th Republic Day on 26th January 2024. The constitution of India was adopted on 26th November 1949 and came into effect on 26th January 1950.

  • Why do we celebrate Republic Day?

    • Republic Day is a national holiday in India, when the country marks and celebrates the date on which the Constitution of India came into effect on 26, January 1950, replacing the Government of India Act (1935) as the governing document of India and thus, turning the nation into a newly formed republic.

    The constitution of India is a huge document which lays down procedures, powers, duties, fundamental rights and directive principles of Government of India & Indian citizens.


  • What do we do on the Republic Day

    • The Republic Day Parade is led by the three divisions of the Indian Armed Forces – Navy, Air Force and the Indian Army. The flags are hoisted all over the India

    Art Workshop,Skits and Dramas,Fancy Dress Competition and Sports,Patriotic Songs and Speeches,Theme based Stage Shows,Small Miscellaneous Ideas. These things are done on Republic day in School.


  • Freedom Fighters of Republic Day

    • Shubhash Chandra Bose, Bhagat Singh ,Mahatma Gandhi, Bal Gangadhar Tilak and many more were the freedom fighters who fought for our country

    The Indian independence movement which was started by Mangal Pandey was a series of historic events with the ultimate aim of ending British rule in India. It lasted from 1857 to 1947


  • Some fact about Republic Day in India
    1. The Republic Day celebrations in New Delhi is a three-day affair that comes to an end on January 29th with the Beating Retreat.
    2. The Indian Constitution, till date, is the longest among all constitutions that exist.
    3. On the eve of Republic Day, India’s highest military and civilian honours are announced
    4. The Indian Air Force came into existence as an independent body on Republic Day
